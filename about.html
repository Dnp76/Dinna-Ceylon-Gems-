Our Heritage

30 Years of Excellence

From the Mines of Ratnapura

Sri Lanka, known historically as "Ratna-Dweepa" or the Island of Gems, has been the source of the world's most magnificent sapphires for centuries. Kings, Queens, and Emperors have adorned themselves with Ceylon stones.

Dinna Ceylon Gems continues this royal tradition. Our founder established the company with a singular vision: to connect the ancient mines of Sri Lanka directly with the world's connoisseurs, eliminating middlemen and ensuring absolute authenticity.

"We don't just sell gems; we are custodians of nature's most brilliant art."
